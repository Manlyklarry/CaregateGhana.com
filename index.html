Why the need for homecare 

          Loneliness and Isolation Among the Elderly:
        Many older individuals experience social isolation
         due to limited time and attention from family
          members, who often juggle busy work schedules 
          and daily responsibilities.
          
         Rising Prevalence of Chronic Illnesses:
         The lack of adequate and quality care for the elderly
          contributes to the growing incidence of chronic diseases.
           Additionally, in some parts of Ghana, older individuals
            are unjustly accused of witchcraft due to the common 
            symptoms associated with aging.
            
         Widespread Abuse of the Elderly:
         The elderly often face mistreatment and neglect, 
         as they are frequently left in the care of irresponsible 
         or uncaring individuals who may physically abuse, mistreat, 
         or steal from them.
         
         Inadequate Living Conditions for the Elderly: 
        Many older Ghanaians live in environments that are neither 
        comfortable nor suitable for their needs, lacking proper
         housing or care facilities to ensure their well-being.
