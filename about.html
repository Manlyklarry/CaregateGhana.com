About Us

Welcome to,Caregate Ghana

Bridget Mawuse Lartey - CEO of CareGate Ghana LTD

As the CEO of CareGate Ghana, I am dedicated to providing compassionate, high-quality care to individuals in need. With over 12 years of experience in caregiving, I have worked with clients facing a wide range of health challenges, including Alzheimer’s, Parkinson’s, recovery, dementia, and partial blindness. My journey in caregiving has shaped my commitment to fostering a supportive and respectful environment for both our clients and our team of caregivers. At Caregate Ghana Limited, we believe in empowering individuals to live with dignity and independence while ensuring their safety and well-being. I am proud to lead a team of professionals who share a passion for making a positive impact in the lives of others. Together, we are redefining care with a focus on empathy, reliability, and personalized services tailored to each client’s unique needs. For me, caregiving is not just a profession—it’s a calling. Through Caregate Ghana, I aim to continue making a meaningful difference in the lives of those we serve.

Quote: “Do it with love and compassion” By Bridget.
